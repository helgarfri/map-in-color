After a lot of building (and rebuilding), I'm really excited to finally launch Mic (Map in Color).

Check it out here: https://mapincolor.com

Mic lets you:

  • Create and share custom maps with your own data
  • Discover maps made by other people
  • Save and comment on the ones you like
  • Be part of a growing community of map nerds, data lovers, and curious people

This is just the start. More features soon, like user profiles, map of the week, and more cool datasets.

If you're into maps, data, or just want to try something new, give it a shot.

Feel free to reply with any thoughts or feedback. I'd love to hear from you.
