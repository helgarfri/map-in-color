I’m excited to announce something new: Map in Color’s Official Map Selection — a weekly map created and curated by the Mic team to highlight meaningful data stories through thoughtful design and insight.

We're kicking off with Volume 1: a map showing Defence Expenditure per Citizen across NATO countries in 2024. It shifts the focus from total military power to the financial burden per person — and the results are surprising.

Nordic countries like Iceland and Norway show high per-capita spending, while Eastern members — despite high budgets by % of GDP — rank lower here. It’s a great example of how per-person data can shift perspective.

Explore it interactively at mapincolor.com.

This series is separate from our user-submitted maps — it's a way for us to set a tone and explore ideas using the Mic platform itself. That said, if you're creating maps too, there's a chance yours could be featured in other ways.
