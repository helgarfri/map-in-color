REFUND POLICY

Last updated February 20, 2026

This Refund Policy explains how refunds work for Map in Color Pro ("Pro"). By purchasing Pro, you agree to this Refund Policy.

1. Pro subscription

Map in Color Pro is a monthly subscription currently priced at $4.99 USD per month. We do not offer yearly subscriptions at this time.

2. Cancellation

You can cancel your Pro subscription at any time. After you cancel, your Pro access will remain active until the end of your current billing month. You will not be charged again unless you re-subscribe.

3. Refunds

Subscription payments are generally non-refundable, except where required by applicable law. This includes partial-month refunds, unused time, accidental renewals, or changes in usage.

However, if you believe you were charged in error (for example, a duplicate charge), please contact us and we will review the issue.

4. Payment processor

Payments may be processed by a third-party payment provider (for example, Paddle). Refunds (if approved) are typically issued back to the original payment method. Processing times can vary depending on your bank or payment provider.

5. How to request help

If you have questions about billing, cancellation, or believe you were charged incorrectly, contact us at support@mapincolor.com. Please include the email address used for your account and any relevant payment details (such as the date of the charge) so we can help you faster.
